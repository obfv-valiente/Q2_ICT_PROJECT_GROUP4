Enrollment Information

OB Montessori Center welcomes students from Preschool to Senior High School. Enrollment is subject to availability and completion of requirements.

Enrollment Process

  • Complete the online application form
  • Submit required documents
  • Admission review and assessment
  • Receive confirmation and fee details
  • Pay fees to secure enrollment

Requirements

  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Recent ID photo
  • Report card or school records
  • Learner Reference Number (LRN)

Tuition Fee

Applicable for all levels: Preschool, Grade School, Junior High, and Senior High School
