    Here, you can see the design of each classroom tailored to their respective grade levels, enhanced by design team NAZARENO / LICHAUCO.


    Casa (Ages 3–5)
    Primary Grade School (Ages 6–8)

    Junior High School (Ages 13–16)
    Senior High School (Ages 17–18)


    CASA

    In the Casa program, classrooms utilize colors akin to that of nature to invoke a warm feeling of being out in nature.






    Activities in the Casa program are designed to invoke critical thinking and develop the mind into being a critical thinker.


    PRIMARY

    In the Primary Program, classrooms have bright colors to expose the developing mind to more concepts.






    Activities in the program aim to increase self-dependence and the ability to have confidence in themselves.



    Intermediate

    In the Intermediate classroom, students are exposed towards objects of science and inovation of the likes of Da Vinci's works and Nikola Tesla.






    Activities in the program aim to increase self-dependence and the ability to have confidence in themselves.


    Junior High School

    The Jr. High School classroom has the silhouettes of critically important works from around the world. This is done in a way to inspire students and give them the creativity they need.






    The activities of the Jr. High School program aim to prepare students for their future by teaching them economic independence, social skills, teamwork, and critical thinking skills.



    Senior High School

    Here, in the Senior Highschool classroom, students are kept to a neat and clean environment.Yet, each classroom of the Senior High celebrates one important figure in Philippine Contemporary arts.






    Activities in this final program have flexibility in the career path they may take as they are prepared for their future in Senior High.
